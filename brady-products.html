About Brady Corporation

Brady Corporation is an international manufacturer and marketer of complete solutions that identify and protect people, products and premises.

Brady specializes in workplace safety as well as identification of cables, wires and products. Brady's products help customers increase safety, security, productivity, compliance, and performance with high-performance labels, signs, safety devices, printing systems and software.
